Starting antidepressants can be scary, both from symptoms and from side effects. This can be all the worse in the first few weeks, particularly if you need to try several different medications.

It can be easy to feel alone in this. But others are going through the same - understanding what they're feeling and how they're reacting can be a powerful thing.

By extracting conversations on antidepressants from social users on reddit.com, this app can share sentiments and understanding on antidepressant usage.

Keyword sets are generated on the comment texts via term frequency-inverse document frequency (TF-IDF) filtering. Sentiment scores for comments are assessed using the Stanford CoreNLP toolkit.
